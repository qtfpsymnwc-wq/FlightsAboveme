Trademark notice and how logo display works on FlightsAboveMe

FlightsAboveMe may display airline logos to help users quickly recognize operators.

Trademarks

Airline names, logos, and related marks are trademarks of their respective owners. FlightsAboveMe is an independent project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any airline.

How logos are used

Logos are used for identification and display purposes. When a matching airline logo is unavailable, FlightsAboveMe may show a generic placeholder.

Corrections or removal

If you are a trademark owner and believe a logo or operator identification is incorrect, please contact us. We’ll review and, where appropriate, correct or remove the asset.
